Job Description

We are looking for an PCB Layout Engineer responsible for the Assist in designing and developing PCB layouts. Provide support to PCB engineering and manufacturing units when needed. Develop prototypes for design verification. Prepare PCB layouts and fabrication and assembly drawings..

Job Responsibilities
  • Assist in designing and developing PCB layouts.
  • Provide support to PCB engineering and manufacturing units when needed.
  • Develop prototypes for design verification.
  • Prepare PCB layouts and fabrication and assembly drawings.
  • Analyze and troubleshoot design and fabrication related issues in a timely fashion.
  • Review existing PCB designs and recommend improvements.
  • Recommend new design techniques to ensure circuit reliability and quality.
  • Assist in development of test plans and develop test procedures and fixtures.
  • Monitor and report project status to Manager on regular basis.
Skills
  • Strong maths and IT skills.
  • A creative flair and design ability.
  • Good visual and spatial awareness.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Problem solving.
  • Written and oral communication.
  • Knowledge of low-level C-based libraries is preferred
  • Commercial awareness.
  • Excellent project management skills.
