INDUSTRIAL CONTROLS

To meet the needs of our customers, GTC carries a large inventory of leading brands such as ABB, Alstom, Bently Nevada, Emerson, GE, Honeywell, Rolls Entronic, Siemens, Triconex, Westinghouse and Woodward. From cables and power supply modules, to signal converters and temperature control modules, we have the spare parts you need to keep your existing equipment function for years to come. With GTC, you can save up to 70% on repairs and 50% on replacement parts, against manufacturer’s prices.

GTC carries both unused and remanufactured control parts, including:

Inverters

Circuit Breakers

Relays

Contractors

LCDs

Switches

Cables

Capacitors

Terminal Blocks

When you purchase your spare parts from GTC, you will be supplied with a 24-month Warranty against defects. We also supply a test and certification service, or test and repair service, both of which include a 24-month Warranty.

Many of our parts also qualify for our best-in-market EXTEND™ warranty program, allowing you to lengthen your parts warranty for up to an additional three years.*

If you need assistance or have any questions, let’s connect! Contact us today.

Circuit Control Care assists users of industrial controls by maintaining systems through means of spares and repairs whether current or obsolete.
